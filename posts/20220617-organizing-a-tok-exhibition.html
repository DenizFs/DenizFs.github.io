Organizing a TOK Exhibition

2022-06-17

The last few months have been very exhausting. My classmates and I were planning our school's TOK exhibition, and because our TOK teacher had assigned me, I took on, among other things, the role of accountant as the "appointed" leader.

It is true that teamwork makes the dreamwork, because without the help of my friends I could neither plan it alone nor cope with the stress. Therefore, I would like to sincerely thank everyone involved and my close friends for all their efforts, time and support.

As for some advice: be decisive as a leader, show your teammates that you value their efforts and have a plan for every scenario. The last advice applies especially to accounting, because as students we made our money by organising several fairs and it was never certain how much money we would have. That's why I have five files for accounting that I worked on into the night.

I have to say this: Before this exhibition I never wanted to take on the role of leader or co-leader. So I would like to thank my TOK teacher for making me have this experience, however stressful and tiring it was. Many thanks to my dear friend Elif for everything. Since she has a more expressive personality, I suggest you look at this exhibition from her point of view on her website.
